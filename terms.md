## 3. How packs work

### Pricing
Each pack has a fixed price in US dollars. The price, minimum payout, maximum payout, and tier-level odds are shown on the pack card before you open it.

### Outcomes
When you open a pack, our server selects an item from the pack's pool using a weighted random algorithm. The selection is performed in a SECURITY DEFINER database function that:

- Uses cryptographically secure randomness
- Has the exact item weights stored alongside it (we can't change them mid-spin)
- Records a verifiable result in our ledger before returning to you

We do not adjust outcomes based on player identity, recent history, or any other personal factor. **Past outcomes do not change the probability of future outcomes** — each open is independent.

### What you get
The item shown after your open is recorded in your "vault" with a US-dollar value at the time of pull. You then have three choices:

1. **Sell to cash** at the live spot price for that metal. Credit appears instantly in your AurumX balance.
2. **Keep in vault** indefinitely; value tracks live spot.
3. **Ship physical metal** of equivalent dollar value to a verified address. The metal we ship may be a different specific coin or bar than the one named in the pack — what's guaranteed is the **dollar value** in the named metal, fulfilled from our in-stock inventory at the current spot price.

### No refunds
Once a pack is opened, it cannot be refunded. The randomised outcome is the product you purchased.
